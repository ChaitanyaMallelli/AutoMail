Setup Guide
1
Create a Telegram Bot

Open @BotFather in Telegram, type /newbot and follow the prompts to receive your BotToken.

2
Start Local Tunnel (ngrok)

Telegram needs a public URL. In a separate terminal, run:

ngrok http http://localhost:5128
3
Register the Webhook

Copy the https:// URL from ngrok and paste it into the Set Webhook URL box. Click Register Webhook.

4
Send a Job & Watch the Magic

Open your bot chat, click Start, then send a text job post, LinkedIn screenshot, or PDF. The pipeline will track it live.
